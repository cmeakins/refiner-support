**Privacy Policy**

Refiner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.  
All data you record—including exercises, workouts, measurements, and templates—is stored locally on your device.  

We do not:  
  •	Collect personal information  
  •	Use analytics  
  •	Use third-party tracking  
  •	Share or sell data  
  •	Transmit any data off your device

To delete all data, simply delete the app from your device.

For questions, contact: support@malimasolutions.com
